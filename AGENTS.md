## Question Transcription Policy
- For question papers provided by the user (images/PDF/text), copy the **Question page** content verbatim into the repo.
- Do **not** summarize, trim, paraphrase, simplify, or reword any part of the question text.
- Preserve original wording, order, punctuation, labels, mark splits, and all listed values/data exactly.
- Apply this rule before adding "Topics to Know" and "Solution" sections.
