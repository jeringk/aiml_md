### Course Description

Social Media Platforms, NLP in SMA, Text Summarization, Opinion Science and dynamics, ML/DL in SMA - Community detection, Ethical Social Media, Case Studies - Role of social media in disaster management, SM driven mental health and behaviour Analysis.

### Course Objectives

| No | Course Objective |
|---|---|
| CO1 | To understand social media, different platforms, their challenges and applications |
| CO2 | To apply Natural language Processing, data analytics and ML techniques for social media analytics |
| CO3 | To understand the social media driven information diffusion process, and behaviour analysis |
| CO4 | To apply social media analytics for business decisions such as on-line marketing strategies, obtaining customer insights, making recommendations |

### Modular Content Structure

#### Techniques for Analyzing Social Media Data

1. Social Media Mining: An Introduction - Zafarani, Ch. 1
   - What is Social Media Mining?
   - Challenges in Social Media Mining
2. NLP in Social Media Analytics - Liu
   - Text Summarization
   - Sentiment Analysis
   - Opinion Summarization
   - Document Classification
3. Machine Learning & Traditional Analytical techniques - Zafarani, Ch. 2, 3, 4, 5
   - Graph
   - Network Measures
   - Network Models
   - Data Mining
4. Community and Interactions - Zafarani - Ch. 6, 7
   - Community Analysis
   - Information diffusion in social media

#### Applications of Social Media Analytics

5. Influence and Homophily - Zafarani, chapter 8
   - Measuring Assortativity
   - Influence
   - Homophily
6. Recommendation in Social Media - Zafarani, Ch. 9
   - Classical Recommendation Algorithms
   - Recommendation using Social Context
   - Evaluating recommendation
7. Behaviour Analytics - Zafarani, chapter 10
   - Individual Behaviour
   - Collective Behaviour
8. Other topics
   - Ethics in social media
   - Online reviews for customer insights
   - Social Media Monitoring linked to business decisions
   - Social Media Marketing Strategies
   - Graph Representation Learning
