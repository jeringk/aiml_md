## 13.1 Social Media Monitoring

- **Social media monitoring** (social listening): tracking mentions, keywords, hashtags, and conversations across social platforms to understand what people are saying about a brand, product, or topic
- **Real-time** awareness of brand perception, competitor activity, and market trends

### Why Monitor Social Media?

| Purpose | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Brand health** | Track sentiment and mentions over time |
| **Crisis detection** | Identify negative spikes early and respond |
| **Competitive intelligence** | Monitor competitor mentions and campaigns |
| **Customer insights** | Understand customer needs, pain points, feedback |
| **Trend identification** | Spot emerging topics and conversations |
| **Campaign measurement** | Assess impact of marketing campaigns |

### Monitoring Pipeline

1. **Data collection**: API-based crawling, social listening tools
2. **Filtering**: relevant keywords, hashtags, accounts
3. **Analysis**: sentiment analysis, volume tracking, topic detection
4. **Visualization**: dashboards, time series, word clouds
5. **Alerting**: real-time notifications for anomalies or crises

### Key Metrics for Monitoring

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Volume** | Number of mentions over time |
| **Sentiment** | Positive/negative/neutral ratio |
| **Share of voice** | Brand mentions vs. competitor mentions |
| **Reach** | Potential audience for mentions |
| **Engagement rate** | Likes, shares, comments per post |
| **Response time** | How quickly the brand responds |
| **Influence** | Influence of users mentioning the brand |

## 13.2 Social Media Analytics Tools

### Google Analytics for Social

- Track social referral traffic to websites
- Measure conversions originating from social platforms
- Identify which social content drives engagement and sales
- **UTM parameters**: tag social links for precise attribution

### Other Analytics Platforms

| Tool | Key Features |
|------|-------------|
| **Sprout Social** | Publishing, analytics, social listening, engagement |
| **Hootsuite** | Multi-platform management, scheduling, analytics |
| **Brandwatch** | AI-powered consumer intelligence, trend analysis |
| **Meltwater** | Media monitoring, social analytics, PR |
| **BuzzSumo** | Content analysis, influencer identification |
| **Socialbakers** | AI-powered social media marketing suite |

### Building Custom Analytics

- **APIs**: Twitter API, Reddit API, YouTube API for data collection
- **Libraries**: Tweepy (Python), PRAW (Reddit), NetworkX (graphs)
- **Frameworks**: Apache Spark for large-scale processing
- **Visualization**: Tableau, PowerBI, Matplotlib, D3.js

## 13.3 Social Media Strategy

- **Social media strategy**: a documented plan for how an organization uses social media to achieve business goals

### Strategy Framework

#### 1. Set Goals (SMART)

- **S**pecific: clearly defined objectives
- **M**easurable: quantifiable metrics
- **A**chievable: realistic targets
- **R**elevant: aligned with business goals
- **T**ime-bound: defined timeline

#### 2. Know Your Audience

- Demographics, psychographics, behavior
- Platform preferences
- Content consumption patterns
- Pain points and needs

#### 3. Competitive Analysis

- Who are competitors on social?
- What content performs well for them?
- What gaps can you fill?
- Share of voice analysis

#### 4. Content Strategy

| Content Type | Purpose | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| **Educational** | Build authority | Tutorials, how-tos, infographics |
| **Entertainment** | Increase engagement | Memes, challenges, behind-the-scenes |
| **Promotional** | Drive conversions | Product launches, offers, testimonials |
| **User-generated** | Build community | Customer stories, reviews, reposts |
| **Interactive** | Boost engagement | Polls, Q&A, live sessions |

#### 5. Measure and Optimize

- Track KPIs (engagement rate, reach, conversions)
- A/B test content variations
- Iterate based on data-driven insights
- Monthly/quarterly reporting and strategy reviews

### ROI of Social Media

$$\text{Social Media ROI} = \frac{\text{Revenue from Social} - \text{Cost of Social}}{\text{Cost of Social}} \times 100\%$$

- Challenges: attribution (multi-touch), valuing non-monetary outcomes (brand awareness, loyalty)
- Proxy metrics: cost per click, cost per engagement, customer lifetime value

## Key Takeaways

- Social media monitoring provides real-time brand intelligence and crisis detection
- Analytics tools (Google Analytics, Sprout Social) enable data-driven decision making
- An effective strategy requires clear goals, audience understanding, content planning, and measurement
- ROI measurement is critical but challenging due to attribution complexity
